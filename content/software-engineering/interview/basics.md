## Q-Guide-w1. Week 1 — Foundations?

**Answer:** Learn: Programming Fundamentals (SOLID), Immutability, Operating Systems basics.
Practice: explain SRP with a real service split; write an immutable Money type.
Quiz each topic to ≥80%.

**Key takeaway:** From interview guide (8-Week Learn → Interview Roadmap).

## Q-Guide-w2. Week 2 — Concurrency & Patterns?

**Answer:** Learn: Concurrency, Design Patterns (Factory/Decorator/Singleton/Observer).
Practice: fix a lost-update counter; decorate a repository with metrics.

**Key takeaway:** From interview guide (8-Week Learn → Interview Roadmap).

**Answer:** Drill: hash maps, two pointers, sliding window, BFS/DFS, light DP.
